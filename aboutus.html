About Us

ERACE Cancer is a student group committed to Establishing Race And Class Equality in Cancer. We aim to effect change by simultaneously educate the Stanford community about health disparities within the field of oncology while simultaneously connecting students with opportunities to conduct health disparities research. Eventually, we hope to disseminate information about cancer prevention and early detection to at-risk populations in Santa Clara and San Mateo Counties and help advertise and coordinate the cancer-screening events hosted by Stanford Hospitals and Clinics.
