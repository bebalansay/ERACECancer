About Us

ERACE Cancer is a student group committed to Establishing Race And Class Equality in Cancer.

We aim to effect change by educating the Stanford community about health disparities within the field of oncology and simultaneously connecting students with opportunities to conduct health disparities research and be involved in community outreach efforts. We have partnered with the Stanford Free Clinics and the Stanford Cancer Institute to disseminate information about cancer prevention to at-risk populations in Santa Clara and San Mateo Counties. We also work with these institutions to advertise and coordinate the cancer-screening events hosted by Stanford Hospitals and Clinics.
