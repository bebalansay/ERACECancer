Clinical Research Fellowship

Interested in clinical research? Hoping to work on a project in oncology and epidemiology before applying to medical school? A fourth year medical student at Stanford is currently building a dataset to investigate the relationship between cardiovascular fitness and cancer outcome and looking for undergraduate research assistants to help with the project. Researchers will primarily be responsible for collecting data from patient records and using Stanford REDCap to store and analyze the data. Work will begin this summer and the dataset will be used for multiple publications over the next few years. If interested, please send any questions and a résumé to ktnead@stanford.edu
